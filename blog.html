How to use the Verismo System by Starbucks
October 24, 2016

Since 1971, Starbucks has been roasting, sourcing and blending high-quality arabica coffee for our customers to enjoy. We’re launching the latest innovation in our coffee journey with the newest generation of the Verismo® System by Starbucks, making it easier than ever to enjoy Starbucks at home with just the push of a button. With a new design, enhanced features and expanded coffee and espresso pod variety in stores, this premium brewing system allows you to craft Starbucks brewed coffee, espresso, and caffé lattes and more consistently and conveniently, one cup at a time. Also new is the Verismo® Milk Frother which allows you to froth your favorite milk and craft Starbucks beverages with hot or cold frothed milk. Watch this step-by-step tutorial to learn how to make coffee and espresso with your Verismo V Brewer.

Iced Pumpkin Spice Latte Recipe
December 6, 2016

Ingredients:
2 tablespoons Starbucks® Fontana™ Pumpkin Spice Sauce
1 Starbucks® Espresso Roast Verismo® Pod
½ cup 2% milk
Sprinkle of nutmeg (optional)
Directions:
Add pumpkin spice sauce to a tall glass. Fill glass with ice. Place under the Verismo® System by Starbucks and brew one Verismo® Pod. Stir to combine ingredients. Add milk to the frother, using the whisk attachment, and hold down the button until the light turns blue. When the light turns off, remove the lid and pour cold, foamy milk into your glass. Give the latte one last stir and top it with a sprinkle of nutmeg if desired. Serve and enjoy.

Behind the Scenes: Starbucks Barista Innovation Challenge
January 30, 2017

It almost feels like a scene out of a reality cooking competition. Glasses of all shapes and sizes and an array of ingredients are lined up on the table. The back room at the Starbucks Reserve® Roastery & Tasting Room in Seattle is buzzing with energy. Baristas are stirring, shaking and sampling their creations. They’re getting ready for the Barista Innovation Challenge.

What started as a group of passionate baristas creating and sharing new recipes is now an official monthly competition. In collaboration with the Starbucks Reserve Brand team, baristas at the Roastery are challenged to create a new drink based on that month’s theme. After a few weeks of preparation, they present their creations to a panel of judges. A winner is chosen and the following month, the drink is featured at the Roastery’s Experience Bar.

“The Barista Innovation Challenge is all about the baristas showcasing their creativity and their ability to innovate,” said Steven Delano, a coffee master on the Global Coffee Innovation team and one of the competition’s coordinators. Delano is a former Roastery barista himself.
